Why was Fancytree 2.0 renamed to 'Fancytree'?
Fancytree is a complete rewrite that also led to some bigger (and some subtle) changes in the API. I felt that this would lead to some confusion, when googling for solutions or looking at code samples in Stackoverflow that refer to older versions of Fancytree.
